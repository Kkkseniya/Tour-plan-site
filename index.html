Tony Wood

Stayed 1 March, 2019

Nice hotel, nice staff. Ideally located, I think. A little away from the noisy street that runs along the beach, in an area closed by a barrier and guarded. It looks very cozy and presentable. You leave the hotel in the evening and in a minute or two you find yourself in the center of shopping and entertainment. From the sea, you can return, wrapped in a towel, leisurely, max 3-4 minutes. To the beach Korona - 15-20 minutes. The room has everything that is stated in the descriptions, plus - free bottle of water, slippers, sometimes - a plate of fruit. Every day there are chrysanthemum flowers on the beds. I was satisfied with literally everything. I recommend the hotel to everyone. And I myself will go there again.

Sofy Black

Stayed 12 July, 2019

All rooms are pool view, our rooms have been recently refurbished and they are well made. The bathroom is spacious, with good pressure and hot water. 2 large swimming pools on 2 sides of the hotel. The location is perfect, across the beach along the main Beach Rd and surrounded by shops, restaurants, street food, everything! BangLa Road is only 100 meters away! We ordered lunch from room service when we first checked into our room because we were starving and the price is very reasonable! Next time I stay here, I will go to the pool.

The best thing about this hotel is the location that it is so close to everything and Bangla Road, but far enough away so you can sit back and relax and unwind.
